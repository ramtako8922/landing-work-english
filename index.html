God Son or Daugther

¿How do you are god son or daugther in baby age?

if you want to be a good son or daughter, you must respect your parents above all else. You must also follow the advice they give you because they desire the best for you and know the dangers of life through their experience. Additionally, you should talk with your parents as friends and share with them anything that affects your daily life so they can help you.

¿How do you are god son or daugther in adult age?

Any thing you should do is provide economic support when you become a productive person because you must be grateful for everything they did for you when you were a child.

You must also pay attention to their health because they may become sick in their advanced age. Similarly, you should think about inviting them to travel with you to beautiful places during holidays, as these activities help them break out of their routine.

Behaving well with your brothers or sisters is an important point to being a good son or daughter because your parents dislike seeing their children fight daily.
